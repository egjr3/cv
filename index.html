Automated Cookie Clicker

Have you ever heard about a video game called Cookie Clicker?

It's a videogame where the user clicks on a big cookie on the screen, earning a single cookie per click and you can progress upon purchasing assets that automatically produce more cookies that you will spend on purchasing more assets to produce more cookies and so on. It's a never ending game.

Given that I don't have much time to play it I decided to create a bot that will play for me. Isn't that awesome!
Don't worry, It will play just for five minutes. We all need healthy gaming habits.
